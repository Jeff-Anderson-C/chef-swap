Store all of your recipes that you want to have access to anywhere; share private recipes among chefs in your groups. Recipes are sorted by category and separated by group. You can leave notes on your recipes to tell your group members how you need help improving your recipe, or just tell your story. Your group members can suggest changes and save your recipe to their favorites.

View your recipe and make edits when needed. Update the photo and change the notes as you imporve your dish. Recipes that are not group specific are available for all of the Chef Sourced community to view, share your food and spread the love.

Still working on that dish? Not ready to share with your group or the community? Keep track of it here in the test kitchen. Your test kitchen keeps your work-in-progress recipes seperate from your completed recipes. You can access the edit screen right from the dash so you can get in and make tweeks, then get back to work. When it's ready, ship it off to your recipes and share with your group.
